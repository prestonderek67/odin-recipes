BBQ Grilled Cheese

Description

This BBQ Grilled Cheese is perfect for a late night snack or as a backyard BBQ party starter! Whip them up any way you would do a normal grilled cheese, but they come with a twist!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices sourdough bread
  • 2oz Cooked chicken
  • 2 Slices favorite cheese
  • 1 Tbs butter
  • 2 Tbs favorite BBQ sauce

Directions

  1. Heat a skillet or flat top to medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of both slices of bread
  3. Once cooking surface is at temp, place 1 slice of bread onto surface, butter side down.
  4. Place 1 slice of cheese, chicken, BBQ sauce onto bread, top with last slice of cheese, then add the last slice of bread.
  5. Flip once bottom bread is crispy. Remove when other slice of bread is crispy. Serve and enjoy!
