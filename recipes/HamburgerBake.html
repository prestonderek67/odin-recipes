Hamburger Bake

Description

This hamburger bake is the best thing since sliced bread! Easy to prepare and fast to serve!

Ingredients

  • 1 Can flaky biscuits
  • 1 Lb ground beef
  • 1 8oz bag shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 Cup BBQ Sauce
  • 1 Cup Mustard

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to biscuit cooking temp on package.
  2. Grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  3. Press biscuits into pan until bottom is covered.
  4. Cook biscuits in oven until almost fully cooked.
  5. While biscuits cook, cook ground beef over medium heat until done. Drain.
  6. Mix together ground beef, BBQ sauce, mustard until combined.
  7. Once biscuits are finished, add meat mixture to pan, then spread bag of cheese on top evenly covering surface.
  8. Place back in oven until cheese is melted, serve and enjoy!
